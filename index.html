Mystery on the Mountain is a humorous 3D skiing adventure game in which players become an amateur blogger-journalist investigating the mysterious disappearance of a skiing champion. Explore the Strudel Mountains ski resort, meet eccentric characters, complete thrilling downhill skiing runs, gather coins, and unlock upgrades. Every slope you master and every weird interview you do brings you closer to unraveling the hidden truth. Will you be able to find the champion?
