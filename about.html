About Sad Alien Studios

Welcome to Sad Alien Studios, where I create humoristic, lighthearted games for people who are going through tough times and need a little light in their lives. As an all-round game developer and musician, I focus on crafting cozy, inclusive experiences that offer a moment of joy and escape.

My Story

Games have always been a source of hope throughout my life. I've experienced my fair share of negativity, but I could always count on games to lift my spirits. From an early age, I discovered that losing myself in game worlds could help me feel a lot better. Some might call it escapism, but to me, it can be a lifeline!

Now that I have the skills to create games myself, I want to pay it forward. In a world filled with wars, climate change, and accidentally dropped ice cream cones, I believe we all need a little more joy and coziness.

My Mission

At Sad Alien Studios, I create games that are:

  • Free from violence
  • Filled with humor (but never at anyone's expense)
  • Cozy and cute
  • Inclusive and welcoming to everyone
  • Pure, harmless fun

My goal is simple: to create games that offer a safe space for anyone who, like me, finds comfort and hope in gaming. Whether you're having a tough day or just need a moment of lighthearted joy, I hope my games can be that little ray of sunshine you're looking for.

Join the Journey

Whether you're a fellow game enthusiast, someone going through a tough time, or just looking for some wholesome fun, you're welcome here. Check out my projects, try my synthesizer, or shoot me an email! - I'd love to hear from you!
